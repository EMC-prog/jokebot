Jokebot privacy policy

Jokebot won't obtain any of your personal information, like your real name or phone number. The only things that we obtain are:

  • Discord Username: Diagnosing in case there are problems.
  • The action done by the bot (and url in case it's an image): Diagnosing to fix bugs.

This data will not be used for advertising or any other case, except the ones put in the list.
